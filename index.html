Portfolio

A mapping portfolio designed by Michaella Mullins

Historic Louisville

This map shows the historical locations of Louisville, comparing and older map of the city with an updated map through Mapbox. Data was processed using QGIS. Data was taken from Data.LouisvilleKY website.

Local College Student Spots, Lexington, KY

This map features locations that I frequent when I am on the University of Kentucky's campus. Data points were taken from Google Maps and cleaned in GeoJson.io.

US Wind Turbines, 2020

This map shows the different wind turbines being built or anticipated to be built in the year 2020. The map was developed using Javascript and Leaflet element.s Data was taken from USGS.

US Rental Rates

This map shows the average rental rates in the US by county. The map was developed using Javascript and Leaflet elements. Data was taken from American Fact Finderand the US Census Cartographic Boundary Shapefiles.
